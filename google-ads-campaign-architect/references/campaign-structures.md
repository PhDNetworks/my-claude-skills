# Campaign Structures by Industry

## Structure Philosophy

For local service businesses with £500-2000/month budgets:
- **Single campaign** with service-based ad groups
- Keeps budget flexible across services
- Easier to manage and optimise

For larger budgets (£2000+/month):
- **Multiple campaigns** by service category
- Allows budget allocation by priority
- Better for businesses with distinct service lines

## Electrician

### Single Campaign Structure
```
Campaign: Electrician Services - Leeds
├── Ad Group: Emergency Electrician
│   └── Focus: 24/7, urgent, same day
├── Ad Group: Domestic Electrician
│   └── Focus: home, house, residential
├── Ad Group: Rewiring
│   └── Focus: rewire, full rewire, house rewire
├── Ad Group: Consumer Unit
│   └── Focus: fuse box, consumer unit, electrical panel
├── Ad Group: EV Charger Installation
│   └── Focus: EV charger, electric car charger, home charger
├── Ad Group: EICR
│   └── Focus: EICR, electrical inspection, landlord certificate
├── Ad Group: Commercial Electrician
│   └── Focus: commercial, office, shop, business
└── Ad Group: Outdoor Lighting
    └── Focus: garden lighting, security lights, outdoor
```

### Multi-Campaign Structure (Higher Budget)
```
Campaign 1: Emergency Electrical - Leeds (Priority, higher budget)
Campaign 2: EV Charger Installation - Leeds (Growth service)
Campaign 3: Domestic Electrical - Leeds (Core services)
Campaign 4: Commercial Electrical - Leeds (B2B)
```

## Naming Conventions

### Campaign Names
`[Service Category] - [Location] - [Optional: Campaign Type]`

Examples:
- "Electrical Services - Leeds"
- "Emergency Roofing - West Yorkshire"
- "EV Charger Installation - Leeds - Brand"

### Ad Group Names
`[Core Service/Keyword Theme]`

Examples:
- "Emergency Electrician"
- "Boiler Installation"
- "Silicone Render"

Keep names descriptive but concise - you'll thank yourself during reporting.
