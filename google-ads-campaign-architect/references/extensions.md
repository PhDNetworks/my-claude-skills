# Ad Extensions Reference

## Minimum Extensions Required

Every campaign should have:
- ✅ Sitelink extensions (4 minimum, 8 recommended)
- ✅ Callout extensions (4 minimum, 8 recommended)
- ✅ Structured snippet extensions (2 minimum)
- ✅ Call extension
- ✅ Location extension (if GBP linked)
- ⭐ Image extensions (recommended)

---

## Call Extension

**Setup:**
- Use primary business phone number
- Enable call reporting
- Set call hours to match business hours
- Consider call-only ads for mobile

**Best Practice:**
- Trackable number if possible (for conversion tracking)
- Ensure someone answers during set hours
- Update if hours change seasonally

---

## Location Extension

**Requirements:**
- Linked Google Business Profile
- Verified business location
- Consistent NAP across listings

**When to Use:**
- Businesses with physical premises customers visit
- Service areas with a base location
- Builds local trust signals

---

## Image Extensions

**Specifications:**
- Minimum 4 images
- Square (1:1) aspect ratio recommended
- 1200 x 1200 pixels ideal
- Must be relevant to ad content

**Image Ideas by Trade:**
- Team photos (branded workwear)
- Completed work examples
- Vehicles with branding
- Accreditation logos
- Before/after transformations

**Avoid:**
- Stock photos (look generic)
- Logos only (use callouts instead)
- Low resolution images
- Cluttered compositions
