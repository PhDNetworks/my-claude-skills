## 30-Day Optimisation Plan

### Week 1
- Monitor search terms daily
- Add negatives immediately
- Drop bids on poor CTR/high CPC keywords
- Reallocate CPC caps to top converters

### Week 2
- Optimise ad rotation for highest CTR headlines
- Remove zero-impression keywords
- Shift CPCs toward best exact match keywords

### Week 3
- Adjust ad schedule based on conversion windows
- Test control vs variant RSA for Hygienist ad group

### Week 4
- Evaluate CPL & CVR
- Tighten negatives further
- Increase mobile bid if >60% conversions from mobile
- Prepare report: leads, CPC, CTR, CVR, CPL
