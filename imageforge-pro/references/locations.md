## Usage Patterns

**Single location:** `-leeds`, `-bradford`
**Area specification:** `-horsforth-leeds`, `-shipley-bradford`
**Regional:** `-west-yorkshire`, `-yorkshire`

## Alt Text Location Patterns

- "...in Leeds"
- "...in Leeds, West Yorkshire"
- "...in Horsforth, Leeds"
- "...serving Leeds and West Yorkshire"
